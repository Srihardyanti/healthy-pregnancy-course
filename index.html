Chapter 1:
Systemic View

🧬

Learn how emotional and social health directly impacts biological pregnancy.

True or False?

Biological changes are the only factor that determines how a pregnancy feels.

Key Insight

Social support and stress management can actually reduce the intensity of physical symptoms like nausea or fatigue.

Chapter 2:
The Challenges

💪

Navigating physical hurdles and emotional shifts in each trimester.

True or False?

Feeling "emotional" or having mood swings is uncommon in the first trimester.

The Challenge

Rapid hormone changes in the first trimester make emotional fluctuations completely normal and expected.

Chapter 3:
Misconceptions

💡

Separating fact from fiction for a healthy lifestyle.

True or False?

A pregnant person must "eat for two," effectively doubling their calorie intake.

Nutrition Fact

You only need about 300-450 extra calories per day in the later stages, not double!
